Privacy Policy

RingBlock

For the Android app com.regexcaller.callblocker.

Last updated: March 20, 2026

What RingBlock does

RingBlock is an Android call screening app. It compares incoming calls against the blocking rules you create and applies the action you chose for matching numbers.

Data RingBlock stores

RingBlock stores your rules, their enabled state, the action for each rule, and local match counts in the app's private on-device database.

Data RingBlock does not collect or share

  • RingBlock does not send your data to a backend service.
  • RingBlock does not include analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.
  • RingBlock does not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • RingBlock does not request `READ_PHONE_STATE`.

Permissions

RingBlock uses Android's call screening role to screen incoming calls.

  • `READ_CONTACTS`: used only to support contact-aware call screening behavior on device.

Import and export

If you choose to export your rules, RingBlock writes the JSON backup file only to the location you select. If you import a backup, the file is read only from the document you choose.

Backup and device transfer

RingBlock excludes its rules database from Android backup and device-transfer flows. Your rules stay on-device unless you explicitly export them yourself.

Network use

RingBlock does not declare the Android Internet permission. Opening this privacy policy happens in your browser, not through an in-app data collection service.

Contact
